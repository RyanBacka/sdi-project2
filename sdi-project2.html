I chose to write a code about the condensation from natural gas that we gather and store as we go through a work day. Condensate is a very valuable liquid that is made from fall out of natural gas. It is used for many things and looks and smells a lot like a low grade gasoline. Most companies make more money from the condensate than from the actual gas extracted.
